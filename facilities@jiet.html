+ CANTEEN

Canteen is not just the place where you go to eat. Especially in colleges and institutes of education, this place is an open arena for debate, argumentative sessions on different issues, cracking gossips, kidding and having loads of fun with cutlets, burgers, tea, coffee and coke in between as gap fillers.

It is the force of double 'f' that drives you to come to a canteen as often as you can. Food and friends- both initiate with 'f' and canteen is the union zone for the two. JIET College canteens build up an ambience of rendezvous where you would love to spend all day with pals keeping away all hassles of mundane living.

The JIET institute has a spacious canteen facility in the college. A variety of south Indian food and snack items are provided to the students and staff of the college with the highest quality standards.

Canteen has a big kitchen and kitchen staff take extra care to provide nutritious and hygienic food in the campus canteen.

+ AUDITORIUM

JIET Group of Institutions has a great auditorium with a seating capacity of 1000 people to promote the conduct of international, national, and other institute events such as conferences, workshops, seminars, meetings, placement events, and cultural events. The auditorium is well-furnished, centrally air-conditioned, and equipped with modern projection and audio systems with access to the internet. It features traditional theatre-style seating, a lifted stage, adjustable lighting, a sound system, a large screen for video and computer presentations, and a vaulted ceiling. It is equipped with state-of-the-art lighting and acoustic systems, audio-visual aids such as microphones, audio/video recording, video playback, slide projector, cue light system, audio/video feeds, etc. The auditorium is created in such a way that they ensure a perfect view of the main stage irrespective of the seating location of the audience.

+ MEDICAL

JIET provides 24x7 health care facilities to the students and staff by providing First Aid and other basic facilities. A full-time qualified Medical Attendant proficient enough to handle medical cases has been appointed to take care of medical concerns, and a doctor is available on call at all times to offer advice and support. The medical room at the campus is well equipped with basic health-related facilities like a thermometer, sterilizer, autoclave, dressing drum, weight measuring machine, B. P. Apparatus, patient bed, wheelchair, stretcher, etc. Regular Health check-ups are organized for the staff with active support from Medipulse Hospital (NABH & NABL Accredited, Jodhpur’s trusted and well-known healthcare provider). In case of complications, the patients are referred to hospitals in the city. Transportation facility is also available round the clock to ensure timely healthcare to the students and staff.
