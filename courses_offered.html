Academic Departments

JIET, an NBA Accredited, NAAC ‘A’ Grade, Autonomous and No. 1 Institute (consecutively three times in a row as per QIV Ranking) by the Bikaner Technical University, has 6 Engineering ( CSE, ECE, ME, EE, CIVIL, Robotics) and one Management ( MBA) branch having well-established strata and equipped with essential equipment to keep education and standard a priority. Moreover, each department has a well-versed staff of the highest qualification in the concerned genre with versatile quality.


The courses of JIET (Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering) are accredited by NBA that enables our students to be eligible in Education and Services in Europe and American Continent.
