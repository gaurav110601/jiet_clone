Autonomous

Jodhpur Institute of Engineering & Technology, Jodhpur has been conferred with Autonomous Status for a period of ten (10) years from the session 2020-2021 to 2029-2030 as per provisions of Clause 3.13 and Clause 6.4 (i) of UGC Regulations dated 12.02.2018 and vide letter no. F. 22-1/2017(AC) dated July 15, 2020, of UGC, New Delhi. With this, JIET has become the First Engineering College of Rajasthan to get the Autonomous Status.
